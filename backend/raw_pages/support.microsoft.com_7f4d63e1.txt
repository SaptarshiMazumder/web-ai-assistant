## Play a meeting recording

After a meeting ends, you can find the meeting's recording in the meeting chat or calendar.

### From a chat

1. Go to **Chat**  ![Teams Chat Tab icon](/images/en-us/3c2a24f3-7e8f-4419-8c78-16c5a795d45f) in Teams.
2. Select the chat for the specific meeting.
3. Select the **Shared** tab.
4. Select a recording.
5. When the viewer opens, select **Play**  ![ Play button](/images/en-us/5ebde09b-2bca-4712-a842-a398a8ae46c2).

   ![Recording screen play selected](/images/en-us/f618b213-3c7c-4e30-a142-3033f0da8f6f)

### From your calendar

1. Open your Teams calendar.
2. Double-click the past meeting you want to see the recording from.
3. Select **Recap**.
4. Select **Play**  ![ Play button](/images/en-us/5ebde09b-2bca-4712-a842-a398a8ae46c2).

You can also select the recording thumbnail in **Details** and select **Play**  ![ Play button](/images/en-us/5ebde09b-2bca-4712-a842-a398a8ae46c2).

### From a channel

1. Select **Teams**  ![Teams button](/images/en-us/20305b5c-df47-4154-a21a-b12712c4224c) in Teams.
2. Select a channel.
3. Select **Files**.
4. Find and select the recording. The recording is an .mp4 file and has the ![Recording button play icon](/images/en-us/e49683ab-bd9f-4750-b2eb-77062f4d7824)**Recording** icon at the beginning of the line.
5. Select **Play**  ![ Play button](/images/en-us/5ebde09b-2bca-4712-a842-a398a8ae46c2) if the recording doesn't start immediately.

## Closed or live captions

Live captions are available during a meeting, but aren't recorded.

To turn on captions during a meeting:

1. In your meeting controls, select **More actions** ![Microsoft Teams more options icon](/images/en-us/cd070cfd-ed96-4162-8b99-a4f89bb3226c) > **Language and speech**  ![Language and speech menu item icon](/images/en-us/16b5b37d-9bd8-4693-8fa8-8daa6c0b9066).
2. Select **Turn on live captions**  ![Closed caption menu icon](/images/en-us/9f04b559-6991-4563-b1a9-ce6277c01f83).

Your captions will appear under the meeting screen. These captions are only shown to you during the meeting.

You can also [start live transcription](/en-us/office/view-live-transcription-in-microsoft-teams-meetings-dc1a8f23-2e20-4684-885e-2152e06a4a8b), which can be viewed after the meeting.

To view closed captions later during playback:

1. Go to **Chat** ![Teams Chat Tab icon](/images/en-us/3c2a24f3-7e8f-4419-8c78-16c5a795d45f) in Teams.
2. Select the past meeting chat.
3. Find and select the recording thumbnail.  
     
   ![Recording thumbnail](/images/en-us/40fe29b0-770f-4416-a3d7-196f581bf175)
4. Hover over the video player and select **Toggle captions** ![Closed caption menu icon](/images/en-us/9f04b559-6991-4563-b1a9-ce6277c01f83), or press **Alt**+**C**.
5. Select your language and view.

If you don't see **Toggle captions**  ![Closed caption menu icon](/images/en-us/9f04b559-6991-4563-b1a9-ce6277c01f83):

1. Select **Open in Stream** ![Open in Stream button](/images/en-us/512217e1-2422-4f54-8edc-ae7a032eac64) at the top of the video.
2. In Stream, select **Video Settings**  ![Stream video settings button](/images/en-us/b35e2738-f872-44d5-b57e-9003f7df8a18).
3. Expand **Transcript and captions**  ![Transcript and captions button](/images/en-us/f596e536-02bf-4904-adac-a269a5edcd6a) > **Generate**.

   ![Stream Generate caption button](/images/en-us/65c88a2f-dd45-4216-9757-7be44b18a54d)

Depending on the length of the video, generating closed captions can take several minutes.

Once generated, you can watch the video in **Stream** and have captions displayed at the bottom of the screen.

When the captions have been created, the **Generate** button turns to **Translate**. Select **Translate** to change the spoken or screen language to automatically translate the captions.

### Customize captions

1. In the video player, hover and select **Playback options**  ![Settings gear button](/images/en-us/c30c71cd-c257-4f10-952c-3566929ca161).
2. Select **Caption settings**.
3. Choose your caption size, color, or background transparency.

**Note:**Captions aren't captured in recordings or downloads. However, you can turn them on while viewing a recording in Teams or Stream.

## Meeting recording storage and permissions

Teams meeting recordings are automatically stored in the organizer's OneDrive for Business.

Meeting recordings expire after a period of time set by your IT admin. Organizers can change recording expiration dates.

To learn more, see [Record a meeting in Teams](/en-us/office/record-a-meeting-in-microsoft-teams-34dfbe7f-b07d-4a27-b4c6-de62f1348c24).

### Channel meetings

For channel meetings, the recording will be stored on the **Files** tab for the channel.

The **Files** tab stores all files for the channel. Everyone who is a part of the channel will have permission to edit and view the recording.

### All other meetings

For non-channel meetings, the recording will be stored in the **Recordings** folder in the meeting organizer's OneDrive folder. If you were invited to the meeting, you can access the recording through the **Files** or **Shared** tabs in the chat, channel, or meeting details.

**Note:**Only the meeting organizer and co-organizers can [edit the recording](/en-us/office/edit-or-delete-a-meeting-transcript-in-microsoft-teams-2f461386-e39a-4444-b9e2-5787da1da11b). But everyone invited to the meeting is able to view the recording.

## Share a link to a meeting recording with others

If you organized the meeting, you can share the recording with people who weren't invited to the meeting by sharing it like you would share any other file.

To share a recording from a meeting chat:

1. Select **Chat**  ![Teams Chat Tab icon](/images/en-us/3c2a24f3-7e8f-4419-8c78-16c5a795d45f) in Teams.
2. Open the meeting chat you want to share a recording from.
3. Select the **Shared** tab.
4. Hover over the recording you want to share and select **More options**  ![Microsoft Teams more options icon](/images/en-us/cd070cfd-ed96-4162-8b99-a4f89bb3226c) > **Copy link**  ![Copy link icon](/images/en-us/7330585a-4674-4aec-a42f-b28a9ba7643f).

   * To manage link access or request the owner to give access to someone outside your org, select **Settings**  ![Settings gear button](/images/en-us/c30c71cd-c257-4f10-952c-3566929ca161) in the **Link** dialog.
5. Paste the link into a message or post to share.

**Notes:**

* Guests and external attendees can only view a recording if they're sent a direct link to the recording.
* To learn more about sharing meeting recordings in OneDrive, see [Share OneDrive files and folder](/en-us/office/share-onedrive-files-and-folders-9fcc2f7d-de0c-4cec-93b0-a82024800c07).

To learn more, see [Customize access to meeting recordings or transcripts in Microsoft Teams](/en-us/office/customize-who-can-access-a-recording-or-transcript-in-microsoft-teams-65869725-a7d7-407c-91d4-8b7b8c8d0d0b).

## Download a meeting recording

Only the meeting organizer can download the meeting recording.

To download a meeting recording:

1. Select the **Files** or **Shared** tab in the meeting chat or channel.
2. Find and select the file.
3. Select **More options**  ![Microsoft Teams more options icon](/images/en-us/cd070cfd-ed96-4162-8b99-a4f89bb3226c) > **Download**  ![Teams download arrow icon](/images/en-us/4c7c01c7-af0f-49f5-aa24-4222f9ce9298).

To learn more, see [Edit or delete a meeting transcript in Microsoft Teams](/en-us/office/edit-or-delete-a-meeting-transcript-in-microsoft-teams-2f461386-e39a-4444-b9e2-5787da1da11b).
