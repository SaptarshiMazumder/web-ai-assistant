* ## Does T-Mobile have a bundle plan?

  Yes! T-Mobile offers a bundle plan that allows you to combine your cell phone service and Home Internet. By bundling these services, you can enjoy the convenience of having everything on one bill and may qualify for exclusive savings every month. We are always adding new ways to save here.
* ## What phone and Internet bundles are available in my area?

  T-Mobile's phone and Internet bundles can vary depending on the Home Internet plan you choose. To find out what options are available in your area, simply use the [eligibility tool](https://www.t-mobile.com/home-internet/eligibility).
* ## How can I figure out which cell phone and Home Internet package is right for me?

  To determine the best package for your needs, consider how much data you typically use, the speed of Internet you require, and how many devices you plan to connect. Our online tool can help you compare different packages based on your preferences. You can also click the chat icon on this screen or visit a local [retail store](https://www.t-mobile.com/stores/locator/) to talk to one of us.
* ## I am an existing customer, can I save when I bundle my cell phone and Home Internet together?

  You bet! If you are already a wireless or Home Internet customer with T-Mobile, we have great deals for you to bundle and save every month on your Home Internet plan.
* ## Is T-Mobile Home Internet cheaper with a phone plan?

  Yes! You can get a discount when you bundle Home Internet with a qualifying phone plan.
* ## How much is T-Mobile Home Internet device connection fee?

  The device connection fee for T-Mobile Internet is typically $35. However, we often run promotions that may waive this fee, so be sure to check with us for any current offers.
* ## If I'm new to T-Mobile, how do I get the cell phone plus Home Internet bundle?

  If you're switching to T-Mobile, getting the cell phone plus Home Internet bundle is seamless! Simply sign up for both a T-Mobile phone plan and Home Internet service, and you'll automatically receive our best deals on Home Internet. Check out the latest deals [here](https://www.t-mobile.com/home-internet/deals).
* ## Can I bundle phone and Home Internet for additional savings if I already have a different discount?

  In many cases, you can combine your current discounts with our bundling offers for even more savings. Check with our customer service team to see how your existing discounts can stack with our bundling options.
* ## How much can I save if I have both phone and Home Internet with T-Mobile?

  The amount you save can vary based on the plans you choose, but customers who bundle both phone and Home Internet services get the best deals on their Home Internet service. Check out the latest deals [here](https://www.t-mobile.com/home-internet/deals).
