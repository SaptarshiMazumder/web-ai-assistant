* ## What is Mesh?

  Mesh uses an access point, to extend the Wi-Fi signal around your house. Unlike extenders that just repeat signals, mesh systems are intelligently create a mesh network that actively manages connections to help remove dead spots and areas with weak signal throughout the home. In addition, a mesh network can provide a more seamless Wi-Fi connection, regardless of where you move your Wi-Fi devices throughout your home.

  With Mesh technology’s ability to adapt and efficiently work all the time, you can have a smoother experience covering smart home needs across devices.

  Mesh can be a good fit for large, multi-story homes or any space that needs an extra boost, since the signals can reach down and around hallways, upstairs or even in the backyard.
* ## Can I still use my existing Wi-Fi router with a Mesh Wi-Fi system?

  It depends on the specific Mesh Wi-Fi system you're using. Some systems require you to use their specific router, while others allow you to use your existing router or replace an existing router’s Wi-Fi network. You should check the product specifications before purchasing a Mesh Wi-Fi system to see if your existing router is compatible.

  With the T-Mobile All-In Home Internet plan, the T-Mobile Wi-Fi Mesh Access Point is optimized for, and only compatible with, select T-Mobile 5G Gateways.

* ## What is Advanced Cyber Security?

  Advanced Cyber Security goes beyond the standard security protections included for all 5G Internet users. With Advanced Cyber Security, customers get proactive monitoring, which helps block threats on your home network and connected devices, preventing cybercriminals from infecting your devices with malware, ransomware and viruses. It also helps protect your home Wi-Fi connected devices from malicious activities, potential threats and invasion — all of which is easily managed through the [T-Life app](https://www.t-mobile.com/apps).
* ## How do I access Advanced Cyber Security?

  Advanced Cyber Security can be managed in the T-Life app.

  Make sure to log in with T-Mobile ID. You may need to update your Gateway firmware the first round. Make sure your T-Life app is updated to the latest version in the Apple app store or Google play store.
* ## What are the types of threats I’m protected from with Advanced Cyber Security?

  There are 4 types of Advanced Cyber Security threats:

  1. Safe browsing helps prevent you from accessing malicious links and landing on risky websites that are known to infect your devices with viruses, ransomware or malware. It helps protect your network, devices and users from phishing attempts for your personal information.
  2. Malicious traffic protection helps prevent your devices from interacting with an IP address that’s known for malicious activities.
  3. Anomaly Detection can recognize when your connected home devices are exhibiting irregular network traffic patterns and suspicious behaviors and automatically blocks that network traffic and cuts off access, deterring cybercriminals from accessing your security cameras and IoT devices.
  4. Botnet prevention provides a second layer of protection against cybercriminals from utilizing your devices to participate in malicious activities across the internet, which could slow down your home network.
