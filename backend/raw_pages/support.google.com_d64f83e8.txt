# Change Google Calendar settings

To change the way Calendar looks or works, update your settings.

Computer AndroidiPhone & iPad

## Update your settings

1. On your computer, open [Google Calendar](https://www.google.com/calendar).
2. At the top right, click Settings ![](//lh3.googleusercontent.com/esYvnQYqTY6dRiUgxyCc8kInS5FCVNfiYr_o9fDccm9xtXYU5Oq_z7eDAH-iIfbqMg=h36) ![and then](//lh3.googleusercontent.com/3_l97rr0GvhSP2XV5OoCkV2ZDTIisAOczrSdzNCBxhIKWrjXjHucxNwocghoUa39gw=w36-h36) **Settings**.
3. On the left, click **General**.

[Open Settings](https://calendar.google.com/calendar/u/0/r/settings)

## Learn about general settings

| **Setting name** | **Description** |
| --- | --- |
| **Language and region** | Change the display language, region, and date and time format on your calendar. [Learn more about language settings](/calendar/answer/50640). |
| **Time zone** | Choose a primary time zone for your calendar. You can also set up a secondary time zone. [Learn how to change time zones](/calendar/answer/37064). |
| **World clock** | In the main menu of Calendar, you can show the current time in different locations around the world. This makes it easier to [schedule meetings across time zones](/calendar/answer/37064). |
| **Event settings** | When you create a new event, you can set a default duration and guest permissions. You can also [manage incoming invitations from senders](/calendar/answer/13159188). |
| **Notification settings** | Change notification settings and sounds for upcoming events. [Learn how to change notifications](/calendar/answer/37242). |
| **View options** | Update the look of your calendar, such as [the first day of your week](/calendar/answer/6110849). |
| **Google Workspace smart features** | Choose to personalize your experience with smart features across Workspace and other Google products. Workspace includes Google apps for businesses and school, such as Calendar, Gmail, Chat, Meet, and Drive. [Learn about Google Workspace smart features](/calendar/answer/15604322).    After you turn on smart features, you can allow Gmail to automatically create events based on flight confirmations, restaurant reservations, and more. [Learn about events from Gmail](/calendar/answer/6084018). |
| **Working hours & location** | If you have a work or school account with an eligible Google Workspace subscription, you can let others know your availability when they try to schedule a meeting with you. [Show your working hours and location](/calendar/answer/7638168). |
| **Appointment schedules** | If you have a work or school account with an eligible Google Workspace subscription, after you connect to Stripe, you can [require payments for appointments that you create](/calendar/answer/13762729). |
| **Keyboard shortcuts** | To navigate around your calendar, use your keyboard. To show a list of available shortcuts, on your keyboard, press **?**. [Learn more about keyboard shortcuts](/calendar/answer/37034). |
| **Offline** | Use your primary calendar when you’re offline. You can’t use other calendars or Tasks without an internet connection. [Learn how to use Calendar offline](/calendar/answer/1340696). |
