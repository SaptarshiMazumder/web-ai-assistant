Schedule and join meetings in Microsoft Teams to work together seamlessly and efficiently anytime, anywhere.

## Join a Teams meeting from anywhere

Whether you’re in the office or on the go, join a Teams meeting anywhere using any device.

There are several ways to join a Teams meeting. You can:

* [Join by link from the meeting invite](/en-us/office/join-a-meeting-in-microsoft-teams-1613bb53-f3fa-431e-85a9-d6a91e3468c9#bkmk_link)
* [Join from calendar or ID](/en-us/office/join-a-meeting-in-microsoft-teams-1613bb53-f3fa-431e-85a9-d6a91e3468c9#bkmk_calendar)
* Join from a [chat](/en-us/office/join-a-meeting-in-microsoft-teams-1613bb53-f3fa-431e-85a9-d6a91e3468c9#bkmk_chat) or [channel](/en-us/office/join-a-meeting-in-microsoft-teams-1613bb53-f3fa-431e-85a9-d6a91e3468c9#bkmk_channel)
* [Call into the meeting](/en-us/office/join-a-meeting-in-microsoft-teams-1613bb53-f3fa-431e-85a9-d6a91e3468c9#bkmk_call)

Before you enter a meeting, choose to [join anonymously or from a different account](/en-us/office/join-a-meeting-in-microsoft-teams-1613bb53-f3fa-431e-85a9-d6a91e3468c9#bkmk_join_different_account), [try out video filters](/en-us/office/apply-video-filters-in-microsoft-teams-meetings-c04a1326-7f63-4170-a4f7-0778520af465), and more from the pre-join screen.

To learn more, see [Join a meeting in Microsoft Teams](/en-us/office/join-a-meeting-in-microsoft-teams-1613bb53-f3fa-431e-85a9-d6a91e3468c9).

## Schedule right in your Teams calendar

Quickly [schedule a meeting](/en-us/office/schedule-a-meeting-in-microsoft-teams-943507a9-8583-4c58-b5d2-8ec8265e04e5) directly in your Teams calendar. Simply select **Calendar** ![Teams Calendar tab icon](/images/en-us/2975b9cc-8d8c-4cec-8585-63cadfca9b49) in Teams, select a time slot, and invite participants. Select **Scheduling Assistant** at the top of the scheduling form to find a time that works for everyone.

![Screenshot of Scheduling Assistant time zone view in Teams](/images/en-us/46f8c151-3a94-4b54-8286-aa1d690566d6)

You can also add details like the meeting agenda for others to review beforehand or [customize meeting options](/en-us/office/meeting-options-in-microsoft-teams-53261366-dbd5-45f9-aae9-a70e6354f88e) to suit your needs.

To learn more, see [Schedule a meeting in Microsoft Teams](/en-us/office/schedule-a-meeting-in-microsoft-teams-943507a9-8583-4c58-b5d2-8ec8265e04e5).

## Start an instant meeting

Need to meet spontaneously or for something urgent? Select **Meet now** ![Icon for Meet Now in Microsoft Teams](/images/en-us/d2f259b5-e638-445a-b880-aec142c6c9f6) at the top of your calendar, a group chat, or a channel to start an instant meeting.

To learn more, see [Start an instant meeting in Microsoft Teams](/en-us/office/start-an-instant-meeting-in-microsoft-teams-ff95e53f-8231-4739-87fa-00b9723f4ef5).

## Send messages in the meeting chat

Meeting chats are chats that are specifically tied to your meeting, making it easy to keep track of discussions and important information.

Select **Chat** ![Teams Chat Tab icon](/images/en-us/3c2a24f3-7e8f-4419-8c78-16c5a795d45f) in your meeting controls to view and participate in the conversation. To stay engaged, [react to others’ messages](/en-us/office/send-an-emoji-gif-or-sticker-in-microsoft-teams-174248c9-e64d-4de1-9f41-3199cc0751ad) or **@mention** specific people in your message to draw their attention. Return to any meeting chat later by selecting **Chat** ![Teams Chat Tab icon](/images/en-us/3c2a24f3-7e8f-4419-8c78-16c5a795d45f) in Teams.

To learn more, see [Chat in Microsoft Teams meetings](/en-us/office/chat-in-microsoft-teams-meetings-64e2cb91-8a11-4781-94ea-fbb23f2b922f).

## Share your video

Turn on your camera before or during a meeting to make communication more personal and foster deeper connections.

While you’re in a meeting, select the dropdown arrow next to **Camera** ![teams show video icon](/images/en-us/35a649ad-9136-422e-a5b2-24beb63cf473) in the meeting controls to preview your video feed and apply video effects, like [filters](/en-us/office/apply-video-filters-in-microsoft-teams-meetings-c04a1326-7f63-4170-a4f7-0778520af465) or backgrounds. When you’re ready to start sharing your video, select **Camera** ![teams show video icon](/images/en-us/35a649ad-9136-422e-a5b2-24beb63cf473) in the meeting controls.

To learn more, see [Use video in Microsoft Teams](/en-us/office/use-video-in-microsoft-teams-3647fc29-7b92-4c26-8c2d-8a596904cdae).

## Present in meetings

![Screenshot of a presenter's view while sharing their screen, with a minimized meeting window next to their shared content.](/images/en-us/f3888c58-ebec-432c-9eaf-984e605435bb)

Share your screen, present slides, or work together directly in documents during a meeting. To start presenting, select **Share**  ![Share screen button](/images/en-us/36dcee31-55d2-4a2c-b993-5ee9bffdb417)in your meeting controls.

Use presentation tools to highlight important points, [annotate](/en-us/office/use-annotation-while-sharing-your-screen-in-microsoft-teams-876ba527-7112-437e-b410-5aec7363c473), and collaborate on content in real-time.

To learn more, see [Present content in Microsoft Teams meetings](/en-us/office/present-content-in-microsoft-teams-meetings-fcc2bf59-aecd-4481-8f99-ce55dd836ce8).

## Host large events

Schedule a town hall or webinar in Teams to host high-capacity events where information is being shared with larger audiences. [Livestream high-quality content](/en-us/office/use-rtmp-in-in-microsoft-teams-789d6090-8511-4e2e-add6-52a9f551be7f), [engage in Q&A](/en-us/office/q-a-in-microsoft-teams-f3c84c72-57c3-4b6d-aea5-67b11face787) with attendees, and much more. Keep track of important event insights before and after the event with attendance and registration reports.

To learn more, see [Get started with Microsoft Teams webinars](/en-us/office/get-started-with-microsoft-teams-webinars-42f3f874-22dc-4289-b53f-bbc1a69013e3) and [Get started with town hall in Microsoft Teams](/en-us/office/get-started-with-town-hall-in-microsoft-teams-33baf0c6-0283-4c15-9617-3013e8d4804f).

## Meeting recap

Meeting recaps are available after recorded or transcribed meetings and are great for catching up on meetings you missed or want to review.

In a meeting recap, you can find the meeting recording and transcript, shared notes and files, and more. Recaps also include the agenda and any follow-up tasks to help everyone stay on the same page.

To learn more, see [Meeting recap in Microsoft Teams](/en-us/office/recap-in-microsoft-teams-c2e3a0fe-504f-4b2c-bf85-504938f110ef).
