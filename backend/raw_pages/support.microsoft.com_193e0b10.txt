You can save a meeting as a draft on your calendar if you aren't ready to send the meeting invites.

Select a tab option below for the version of Outlook you're using. [What version of Outlook do I have?](https://support.microsoft.com/office/what-version-of-outlook-do-i-have-b3a9568c-edb5-42b9-9825-d48d82b2257c)

New OutlookClassic OutlookWeb

**Note:**If the steps under this **New Outlook** tab don't work, you may not be using new Outlook for Windows yet. Select **[Classic Outlook](#picktab=classic_outlook)** and follow those steps instead.

### Save a meeting as a draft in new Outlook

1. Select **Calendar** from the navigation and create a **New event**.
2. Select the **Send** dropdown and select **Save as draft**.

   **Note:**The drop-down will only appear when at least one participant is added to the invite. Note that the draft is not stored in your mailbox draft folder.

   ![Outlook Calendar ](/images/en-us/9e7c78d2-c6db-4f8e-8fee-b71570239a40)
3. This will save the meeting invite on your calendar. It will show on your calendar as **[Draft]** along with the title of the event.

### Edit the draft

When you are ready to edit the draft meeting and send, do the following:

1. Locate the draft meeting on your calendar and select **Edit**.  
     
   ![Draft Sales Meeting from Calendar](/images/en-us/877ef4d5-af16-4a6c-9ab2-789429901115)
2. Make your changes and select**Send**. You can also save it as a draft again if you're not ready to send it.

### Save a meeting as a draft in classic Outlook

1. Select **Calendar** from the navigation and select **New Appointment**.
2. Fill out the details and if you didn't add any attendees, select Save & Close. If you added at least one attendee, close the meeting request.
3. From the pop-up window, select one of the options such as **Save changes but don't send**.
4. Select **OK**.

### Edit the draft

When you are ready to edit the draft meeting and send, do the following:

1. Locate the draft meeting on your calendar and select **Edit**.
2. Make your changes and select**Send**. You can also save it as a draft again if you're not ready to send it.

### Save a meeting as a draft in the web version of Outlook

1. Select **Calendar** from the navigation and create a **New event**.
2. Select the **Send** dropdown and select **Save as draft**.

   **Note:**The drop-down will only appear when at least one participant is added to the invite. Note that the draft is not stored in your mailbox draft folder.

   ![Outlook Calendar ](/images/en-us/9e7c78d2-c6db-4f8e-8fee-b71570239a40)
3. This will save the meeting invite on your calendar. It will show on your calendar as **[Draft]** along with the title of the event.​​​​​​​

### Edit the draft

When you are ready to edit the draft meeting and send, do the following:

1. Locate the draft meeting on your calendar and select **Edit**.  
     
   ![Draft Sales Meeting from Calendar](/images/en-us/877ef4d5-af16-4a6c-9ab2-789429901115)
2. Make your changes and select**Send**. You can also save it as a draft again if you're not ready to send it.
