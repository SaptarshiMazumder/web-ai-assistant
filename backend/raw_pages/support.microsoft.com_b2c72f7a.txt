Enable attendance reports to view and download important attendance details, like who attended and what time people joined and left, during and after your meeting. Only organizers and co-organizers can view and download attendance reports.

DesktopMobile

## Turn attendance reports on or off

Organizers can turn attendance reports on or off in meetings they create.

### Before a meeting

To turn attendance reports on or off before a meeting, first select the Teams calendar version you’re using. To learn more, see [Get started with the new calendar in Microsoft Teams](/en-us/office/get-started-with-the-new-calendar-in-microsoft-teams-98f3b637-5da2-43e2-91b3-f312ab3e4dc5).

### New calendar

To turn attendance reports on or off:

1. Select **Calendar** ![Teams Calendar tab icon](/images/en-us/2975b9cc-8d8c-4cec-8585-63cadfca9b49) in Teams.
2. Select an event and select **Edit** ![Edit icon](/images/en-us/073d9d9a-0d84-402f-acdb-5765e790261f) > **More options**.
3. In **Event**, select **Meeting options** ![Settings icon](/images/en-us/e2a1f329-d95b-7d5e-181c-7c3e12fd9321).
4. In **Participation** ![Icon for Participation in Microsoft Teams](/images/en-us/e56db9d7-f31f-4025-a075-69f9d96b1c85), turn the **Allow attendance report** toggle on or off.
5. Select **Save**.

For more help managing meeting attendance reports, contact your IT admin.

### Classic calendar

To turn attendance reports on or off:

1. Select **Calendar** ![Teams Calendar tab icon](/images/en-us/2975b9cc-8d8c-4cec-8585-63cadfca9b49) in Teams.
2. Select a meeting or event.
3. Select **Edit**.
4. In the meeting description, press and hold **Ctrl** and select **Meeting options**.
5. In **Participation** ![Icon for Participation in Microsoft Teams](/images/en-us/e56db9d7-f31f-4025-a075-69f9d96b1c85), turn the **Allow attendance report** toggle on or off.
6. Select **Save**.

For more help managing meeting attendance reports, contact your IT admin.

### During a meeting

To turn attendance reports on or off during a meeting:

1. Start a Teams meeting.
2. Select **More actions** ![Microsoft Teams more options icon](/images/en-us/cd070cfd-ed96-4162-8b99-a4f89bb3226c) > **Settings** ![Settings button](/images/en-us/fe3fb52d-f1db-49e6-88cf-7d1ec1e8022e) > **Meeting options** ![Meeting options icon](/images/en-us/f788f885-730d-4417-99a6-6e08972affce) in your meeting controls.
3. In **Engagement**  ![Teams Chat Tab icon](/images/en-us/3c2a24f3-7e8f-4419-8c78-16c5a795d45f) turn the **Allow attendance report** toggle on or off.
4. Select **Save.**

## View and download attendance reports

View and download Teams attendance reports directly in Teams after an event ends. Reports are stored along with the organizer’s other data, like emails and contacts.

Organizers and co-organizers can view and download attendance reports in the event chat about five minutes after the event ends. Recurring meetings will have a unique attendance report for each instance of the series.

To view and download an attendance report:

1. Select **Chat** ![Teams Chat Tab icon](/images/en-us/3c2a24f3-7e8f-4419-8c78-16c5a795d45f) in Teams.
2. Open the past meeting or event chat.
3. Select the **Attendance** tab.
4. Select **Download** ![Teams download arrow icon](/images/en-us/4c7c01c7-af0f-49f5-aa24-4222f9ce9298) to download the attendance report as a .csv file.

You can also download an attendance report during a meeting by selecting **People** ![People or Show Participants button](/images/en-us/f74af4a7-4e65-4174-a12a-ae68fa7f00a5) in your meeting controls and selecting **More actions** ![Microsoft Teams more options icon](/images/en-us/cd070cfd-ed96-4162-8b99-a4f89bb3226c) > **Download attendance list** ![Teams download arrow icon](/images/en-us/4c7c01c7-af0f-49f5-aa24-4222f9ce9298) in the **Participants** pane.

**Notes:**

* If the event organizer leaves the org, reports are permanently deleted and can’t be retrieved.
* Co-organizers can only view and download reports from events scheduled in Teams, not Outlook.

## Hide identity in attendance reports

You can decide whether you want your identity shown in attendance reports.

To hide your identity in attendance reports:

1. Go to **Settings and more**  ![Microsoft Teams more options icon](/images/en-us/cd070cfd-ed96-4162-8b99-a4f89bb3226c) > **Settings**  ![Settings button](/images/en-us/fe3fb52d-f1db-49e6-88cf-7d1ec1e8022e) in Teams.
2. Select **Privacy**  ![Privacy settings button](/images/en-us/eadf610d-4172-45d7-8216-7ca723931b30).
3. Turn the **Identify me in attendance reports** toggle on or off.

## Engagement information

Organizers with Teams Premium can view and download meeting engagement data such as total reactions, raised hands, cameras turned on, and more in the **Attendance** tab after a scheduled meeting or webinar.

![Engagement information available in the Attendance tab of a meeting.](/images/en-us/e14dd3b9-9b3d-4795-a426-42d44e26635e)

## Known issues and limitations

* In meetings with more than 120 participants, the attendance report available during the meeting will only include some attendees. The post-meeting report will contain all attendees.
* The report doesn't include insights from deleted breakout rooms.
* The report doesn't currently include view-only attendees.
* If a channel meeting is in progress and someone starts a second meeting within the same channel conversation, an attendance report will be available only for the second meeting.
* If someone waits in the lobby and doesn't get admitted to the meeting, they won't be included in the report.
* When a participant is on hold in a meeting, their info is still included for this period of time in the attendance report.
* Attendance reports aren't supported for instant channel meetings.
* Engagement reports limit how many actions are logged for each attendee (hand raises, mutes/unmutes, etc.).
* Co-organizers can't access attendance reports for channel meetings.

View attendance reports directly in the [Microsoft Teams mobile app](https://www.microsoft.com/microsoft-teams/download-app#office-CustomSpacingTemplate-uoh60pc) to gain insight into your meetings and webinars. See webinar registration data, times that attendees joined and left meetings, and much more.

**Note:**To see all attendance report data, view the report on Teams for [desktop](https://www.microsoft.com/microsoft-teams/download-app#desktopAppDownloadregion) or [web](https://www.microsoft.com/microsoft-teams/log-in).

## Turn attendance reports on or off

To turn attendance reports on or off before your meeting:

1. Tap **Calendar** ![Teams Calendar tab icon](/images/en-us/2975b9cc-8d8c-4cec-8585-63cadfca9b49) in Teams.
2. Tap a meeting.
3. In **Details**, tap **See more** > **Meeting options**.
4. In **Participation** ![Icon for Participation in Microsoft Teams](/images/en-us/e56db9d7-f31f-4025-a075-69f9d96b1c85), turn the **Allow attendance report** toggle on or off.
5. Tap **Save**.

Participants can decide if they want to show or hide their information in attendance reports.

To change your attendance report privacy settings:

1. Tap your profile picture in Teams.
2. Tap **Settings** ![Settings button](/images/en-us/fe3fb52d-f1db-49e6-88cf-7d1ec1e8022e) > **Privacy** ![Privacy settings button](/images/en-us/eadf610d-4172-45d7-8216-7ca723931b30).
3. Tap **Attendance report data**.
4. Turn the **Identify me in attendance reports** toggle on or off.

For more help managing your meeting attendance report settings, contact your IT admin.

## Open an attendance report

To open an attendance report:

1. Tap **Chat** ![Teams Chat Tab icon](/images/en-us/3c2a24f3-7e8f-4419-8c78-16c5a795d45f)​​​​​​​ in Teams.
2. Open the past meeting chat history.
3. In the **Meeting ended** message, tap **Attendance** ![Attendance icon in Microsoft Teams](/images/en-us/4d6b980a-87b2-485c-a900-ee50cc3ee800)​​​​​​​.
